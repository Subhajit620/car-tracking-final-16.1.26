About Our Platform

The Car Tracking System is a modern GPS-based solution that allows vehicle owners to track, monitor, and secure their vehicles in real time. Accurate location, vehicle status, and driver details are displayed on a secure dashboard.

Each vehicle is connected with a GPS device sending continuous location data. Owners can view the exact latitude, longitude, and last updated time.

Security is a key priority. Encrypted passwords and session-based authentication ensure owners access only their vehicles. The system guarantees data privacy and reliability.

Ideal for personal owners, fleet management, transport services, and logistics companies. Enhance safety, reduce risks, and improve operational control with real-time insights.
